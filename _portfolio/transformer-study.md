## Overview

A structured study program on **Transformer architectures** and the **Attention mechanism**, designed and led for members of [Aing](https://github.com/aing-gachon), the AI academic club at Gachon University (가천대학교).

The study was created to help junior members move beyond simply using pretrained models and instead understand how Transformer components work internally. It combines paper reading, paper-to-code mapping, implementation practice, and small-scale experiment tracking.

## Contents

- Transformer architecture (Vaswani et al., "Attention is All You Need")
- Self-Attention and Multi-Head Attention
- Positional Encoding
- Encoder-Decoder structure
- Paper-to-code mapping and concept quizzes
- From-scratch implementation practice with PyTorch notebooks
- Fine-tuning experiments on Multi30k/IWSLT-style translation tasks
- Experiment logging and strategy review sessions
